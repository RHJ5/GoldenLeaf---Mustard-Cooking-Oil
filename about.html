Our Story

For over three decades, GoldenLeaf has been pressing mustard seeds the way it was meant to be — slowly, carefully, and without shortcuts. Our family began with a single stone press and a simple promise: deliver oil so pure you can smell the fields in every drop.

Today we work directly with hundreds of farmers across the mustard belt, supporting sustainable agriculture and traditional cold-press methods. Every bottle that leaves our facility is tested for purity, aroma and clarity.

From your kitchen to ours — thank you for being part of the GoldenLeaf family.
